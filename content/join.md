## Join Us

We are always eager to work with self-motivated students who are passionate about cutting-edge systems research. Our research areas include, but are not limited to:

- High-Performance Computing (HPC)
- Distributed Storage Systems & Parallel File Systems
- Systems for AI/LLMs
- Performance Modeling & Analysis
- Parallel I/O
- GPU & Agentic AI Systems

**PhD applicants.** Candidates must, at minimum, meet NTU's admission requirements. Please refer to the official NTU [admission guide](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/radmissionguide) for details.

**Opportunities for NTU undergraduates.** We co-advise NTU's [Student Cluster Competition team](https://ntuhpc.org). NTU undergraduates interested in supercomputing can join the team to gain hands-on experience with HPC systems and potentially travel to competitions in the U.S. and Germany. This is an undergraduate-only competition — if you're interested in participating, please reach out.

If you're interested in working with us, please contact the PI at <ins>*chen.wang AT ntu.edu.sg*</ins>.
